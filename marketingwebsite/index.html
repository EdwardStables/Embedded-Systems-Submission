Design

Interior

Sensors and Other Components

The temperature sensor allows sensitive packages to be shipped with peace of mind.

Upper and lower allowable limits on the temperature of the package can be specified to give complete customizability and flexibility.

The shock sensor allows fragile goods to be continuously tracked to ensure that they arrive at their destination safely.

The tilt of your package is also constantly recorded so as to alert you immediately of any potential mishandling or damages.

A rechargeable battery allows the device to be used for multiple shipments.

Exterior

Packaging

The internal components used are lightweight to minimize additional costs incurred during shipment and arranged in a compact manner for a slim finish.

With its slim and lightweight design, this device can be easily attached to your deliveries.

The device also utilises a robust design which incorporates a metal frame to provide additional support and protection of the device during shipments.

Indicators on the front of the device provides the recipient with reassurance that their delivery was shipped safely.
